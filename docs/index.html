Welcome!

I’m Christopher Loan, a PhD student at the UO’s Quantitative Research Methods in Education program, a Statistics & Data Science Consultant & a Data Science Mentor at RStudio.

I primarily consider myself a methodologist, exploring applications of structural equation modeling, multilevel-modeling, machine learning, data visualization, & their intersections.

I’ve applied these methods in Prevention Science, Psychology, & Education contexts, with my theoretical interests lying at these fields’ boundaries. I use R every chance I get — including making this website with distill — but dabble in Python, as well.
